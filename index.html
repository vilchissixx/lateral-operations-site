The hiring problem

More resumes rarely fix a messy search.

Most teams do not need a larger candidate pile. They need candidates screened against the actual role, schedule, compensation range, communication expectations, and remote working reality.

Talent Sprint keeps the search narrow on purpose. We define the role, build the scorecard, source intentionally, screen for practical fit, and deliver a shortlist your team can actually review without losing the week.
