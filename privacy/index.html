Privacy

Newsletter privacy, in plain English.

Lateral Operations only adds people to the newsletter when they knowingly request it. Cold outbound and newsletter subscriptions remain separate systems.

What is collected

The newsletter form collects an email address, optional first name, and consent to receive the Lateral Operations newsletter. Basic technical details may be processed by the website host and newsletter provider to submit the form, prevent spam, and deliver email.

Why it is collected

The information is used to send requested newsletter updates about international hiring, remote operations, customer success, sales and support roles, founder operations, and related Lateral Operations notes.

Email provider

The newsletter endpoint is configured to use Mailchimp through server-side environment variables. Mailchimp processes subscriber information for list management, unsubscribe handling, suppression of bounced addresses, campaign sending, consent management, and basic analytics.

Unsubscribing

Every marketing email sent through the newsletter provider must include a visible unsubscribe mechanism. Subscribers can also request removal by emailing alberto@lateraloperations.com.

Deletion requests

To request deletion of newsletter subscriber data, email alberto@lateraloperations.com. Lateral Operations will use the newsletter provider's tools to remove or suppress the subscriber record where required.

Separate from outbound

The Lateral Operations prospect tracker is not imported into the newsletter. Cold-outreach leads are not automatically subscribed. The newsletter is for subscribers, clients, partners, and people who knowingly request the content.
